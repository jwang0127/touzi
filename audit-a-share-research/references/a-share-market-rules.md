# A-share market-rule checks

Load rules applicable to the historical date. Check trading calendars, settlement/turnaround restrictions, board- and period-specific price limits, ST treatment, suspensions, delisting, corporate actions, one-price limit boards, liquidity, commissions, transfer fees, stamp duty, slippage, and capacity.

Block future functions, survivorship bias, same-close execution after close signals, impossible fills, and claims based on zero trading cost.
